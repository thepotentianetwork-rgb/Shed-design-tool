The Story

Where We Come From

Chonis is more than a name — it's a word that lives in the everyday language of Mexican homes, spoken with laughter between cousins, whispered at the laundromat, etched into childhood. It means underwear, yes. But it means so much more.

This brand was born from the experience of living between two cultures — the richness of Mexican heritage and the hustle of American life. A duality that millions carry daily, yet rarely see represented in the spaces where luxury lives.

We set out to change that. To take something intimate and everyday, and elevate it with the pride, craft, and boldness that our community deserves.

"Made for the ones who grew up navigating two worlds — and mastered both."

Chonis · Est. 2025
