We Named It What Your Abuela Called It

Growing up, everybody called them chonis. Your tía, your mom, your abuela yelling from the other room to grab clean ones before school. It's not a dirty word — it's a familiar one. And that's exactly the point.

Chonis isn't just men's underwear. It's for anyone who grew up hearing that word — the whole familia. Born from the intersection of Mexican and American culture, worn by everyone in between.

We took something everyday, something that made you laugh just hearing the name, and made it luxurious. Because why not? La comunidad deserves nice things — even the ones you don't show off at the dinner table.

"You called them chonis. Now they're a whole brand. You're welcome."

— Chonis · Est. 2025
